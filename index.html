Get to Know Us

We are a team of bike enthusists who know the ins and outs of any bike. Vicious Cycles was founded in 2010, and Raleigh, NC is where we call home. From casual bikes to mountain bikes, we bet we'll have one that suits you just right.

If you have a family, don't just bring yourself to our shop-bring the whole family over so everyone has a bike. Vicious Cycles highly encourages family riding, as it's a great way for the family to bond, get exercise, and have fun together.
